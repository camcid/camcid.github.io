PhD application at Cambridge

We are accepting PhD applications from candidates with exceptional data science and writing skills demonstrated through peer-reviewed publications and a passion for climate action. Please get in touch with Dr. Ramit Debnath (rd545@cam.ac.uk) with your research proposal.

Postdoctoral fellowships

We welcome exceptional and visionary postdoctoral researchers with demonstrable high-impact research portfolio. Please get in touch to discuss potential projects, please see some funding opportunities below where CamCID can host the fellows:

British Academy: British Academy postdoctoral fellowship

Leverhulme Trust: Leverhulme Trust early career fellowship

AHRC: AHRC early-career fellowship

Arts and Humanities Research Council: Comprehensive list of funding for Arts and Humanities Research

Economic and Social Research Council: Comprehensive list of funding for Economics and Social Research

Cambridge ESRC partnership funding: ESRC DTP Cambridge fellowship

SNSF Mobility fellowships: SNSF Mobility Fellowship

Note: We are not just limited to above fellowships. If you have independent funding and want to pursue ground-breaking people-centric climate action research, please get in touch.

Internships and Research Assistantships

We always hosts interns and research assistant over summers or during term time depending on research requirement.

Summer internships are available through UROPS (link) or Cambridge Zero Summer Internships (link) for University of Cambridge students. We also hosts remote internships over summer/winter periods or as discussed with director, email Dr Ramit Debnath (rd545@cam.ac.uk) directly.
